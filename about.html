ABOUT US

Our History

Kimberley Cremation & Funerals was established in 2001. We have our own premises and crematorium, as well as private cemetary. We provide a complete flexible funeral or cremation service as per your needs.

With our own crematorium there are no need for the transport of the deceased and no long waiting period for ashes. We cremate every Wednesday, but also on other days if needed. Ashes are available on the same day to inter. At Kimberley Cremation & Funerals you get peace of mind that you, while enjoying our support, will surely save on this necessary cost. OUR STATEMENT

Our mission is to provide the community with professional and quality service during difficult times. We strive to treat the deceased with the utmost care and dignity, while offering empathetic support to their families. Our availability round the clock ensures that we can cater to their needs and address any problems that may arise.

Our vision is to be the trusted and compassionate funeral service provider in the community. We aim to create a supportive environment for grieving families by offering professional assistance and handling all their requests with care and respect.

At our funeral service, we are committed to remaining fully involved in the arrangements of church services, including selecting ministers, coordinating organists, and providing flowers. By directly managing these aspects, we eliminate middlemen and reduce costs, ensuring that families receive personalized and affordable services. Our dedication to professionalism and comprehensive support sets us apart in the industry.
